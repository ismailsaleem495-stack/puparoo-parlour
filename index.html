03 About Ella

Qualified, caring,
one dog at a time.

I'm Ella, a City & Guilds qualified dog groomer based in Copthorne. I offer a full range of grooming services with one thing at the heart of everything I do — your dog gets my complete, undivided attention from the moment they arrive to the moment they leave.

There are no distractions, no other dogs, and no rushing. Every appointment is a 1-to-1 experience tailored to your dog's individual coat, temperament, and needs. Whether your dog is a familiar face or visiting for the first time, they'll always be treated with the same patience and care.

I love seeing my regulars and genuinely enjoy welcoming new dogs into the parlour. If you're looking for a groomer who takes their time and truly cares, I'd love to meet your dog.
